A BMI calculator helps assess body mass index by measuring weight and height, guiding you to a healthy weight range.

BMI Calculator Chart

BMI Weight Status
Below 18.5 Underweight
18.5 - 24.9 Healthy
25.0 - 29.9 Overweight

Calculate your BMI

To calculate BMI, divide your weight in kg by your height in meters squared. A BMI between 18.5-24.9 is healthy.
